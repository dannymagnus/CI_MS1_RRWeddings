a bit about me...

I'm an ocean obsessed artist, food lOver, diver, explOrer, true crime addict and i just lOve making paper lOok pretty!

I’m Rebecca and I run my design studio in my home in Sutton Coldfield, in the West Midlands

Painting is my passion!
I love creating design that are truly unique and personal, my favourite parts of the design process is being able to incorporate meaningful illustrations so it becomes more than a piece of paper.

All of my illustrations are hand painted (by myself!) so you will always have invitations that have been lovingly and passionately crafted. My inspiration comes from nature and the outdoors, and I love to put my paintbrush to paper to create intricate floral designs or beautiful watercolour washes that can be transferred to a set of stationery for your special day.

Head to my bespoke and customisable pages to see my designs and when you’re ready to book or have any questions contact me via email or my contact form.
